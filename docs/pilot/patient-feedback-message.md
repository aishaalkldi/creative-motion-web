# RASQ Patient Feedback Message — WhatsApp / SMS

**Purpose:** Collect lightweight patient clarity feedback after the first controlled clinic demo — without in-app forms or database changes.

**When to send:** 24–48 hours after the patient completes their first portal session (or after assessment if no plan yet).

**How to send:** Copy the Arabic or English block below into WhatsApp or SMS. Ask the patient to reply with the option letters or numbers.

**Privacy:** Do not include clinical details in the message. Use the patient's first name only if clinic policy allows.

---

## Arabic (WhatsApp / SMS friendly)

```
مرحباً [الاسم]،

نود معرفة تجربتك مع برنامج RASQ من معالجك. ردّ باختصار — لا حاجة لجمل طويلة:

1️⃣ هل فتحت الرابط بسهولة؟
   أ) نعم   ب) لا

2️⃣ هل فهمت التمارين؟
   أ) نعم   ب) جزئياً   ج) لا

3️⃣ هل شعرت أن البرنامج مخصص لك من معالجك؟
   أ) نعم   ب) لا

4️⃣ هل ستكمل الجلسة القادمة؟
   أ) نعم   ب) غير متأكد   ج) لا

5️⃣ ما الشيء الواحد الذي أربكك؟
   (اكتب بكلمة أو جملة قصيرة)

شكراً — ردّك يساعدنا على تحسين التجربة.
```

## English (WhatsApp / SMS friendly)

```
Hi [Name],

We'd like to hear about your experience with RASQ from your therapist. A short reply is fine:

1️⃣ Did you open the link easily?
   A) Yes   B) No

2️⃣ Did you understand the exercises?
   A) Yes   B) Partly   C) No

3️⃣ Did the program feel personalised by your therapist?
   A) Yes   B) No

4️⃣ Will you complete the next session?
   A) Yes   B) Not sure   C) No

5️⃣ What is the one thing that confused you?
   (One word or short sentence)

Thank you — your feedback helps us improve.
```

## Reminders for staff sending the message

- Send from the clinic's official number when possible.
- Do not ask for pain scores or clinical details in this message.
- If the patient reports an emergency or severe symptoms, follow clinic protocol — do not handle clinically via chat.
- Arabic first for Arabic-speaking patients; offer English if preferred.
