# RASQ Clinician Feedback Form — Pilot Week

**Purpose:** Collect structured feedback from the first controlled clinic demo before building in-app feedback or dashboards.

**When to use:** Within 24 hours after each pilot session or at end of pilot week.

**How to use:** Ask the clinician aloud, or send as a short form (Google Form, WhatsApp, email). Record answers in `pilot-evidence-log.md`.

**Do not collect patient PHI in free-text fields.** Use first names or patient IDs only if clinic policy allows.
